# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Primary users are medical specialists managing appointments and consultations, patients booking and completing pre-consult questionnaires, and clinic admins monitoring governance and operational metrics.

## Product Purpose
MiDoc is a healthcare SaaS platform that streamlines clinical workflows with AI-assisted SOAP note generation, specialty playbooks, governance traceability, patient communication, and operational dashboards. Success means faster documentation, safer decisions, and clearer operational control for clinics.

## Brand Personality
Reliable, clinically professional, and approachable. The UI should communicate trust, clarity, and control under time pressure.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic “AI startup” aesthetics (neon gradients, glassmorphism, flashy motion), decorative dashboards with hero-metric clichés, and over-carded layouts that hide task flow.

## Design Principles
1. Clinical clarity before decoration: prioritize task completion under cognitive load.
2. Consistent interaction vocabulary: same controls and states across all modules.
3. Progressive disclosure: show essential decisions first, reveal complexity on demand.
4. Trust through feedback: every action has clear status, error recovery, and next step.
5. Mobile competence without compromise: workflows must remain complete on touch devices.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG 2.1 AA at minimum, with keyboard-accessible interactions, visible focus states, meaningful labels, and reduced-motion-safe transitions.
